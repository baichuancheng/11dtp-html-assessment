Our Project

We're working to bring back lost species, not just for curiosity, but to restore balance in ecosystems and protect biodiversity for the future. It matters because some extinct animals played key roles that shaped entire environments, such as the woolly mammoth. Bringing them back can help revive damaged ecosystmes. One important thing is that humanity played a role in many extinctions. De-extinction is a way of making amends and giving back. Also, De-extinction pushes the limits of genetics, biology, and conservation technology. So, join us, you’ll be part of a movement that combines science, ethics, and conservation to change the future of life on Earth, and you will find that it is a meaningful and worthwhile thing to do!
